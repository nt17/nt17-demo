Visa and Invitation Letter


Brazil maintains a fully reciprocal visa policy. Please check what applies for your citizenship at the Immigration division office:
http://www.portalconsular.itamaraty.gov.br/vistos
The NT17 VISA invitation letter will be made by using the ConfTool Conference Administration System. When you create a new user account the Conftool system will ask if you need a Visa Invitation Letter. The NT17 organizers advise you to apply for your visa as early as possible, at least 3 months prior to the conference.
