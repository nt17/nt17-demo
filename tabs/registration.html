Registration, Payment and Abstract Submission

Registration, abstract submission and payment to NT17 will be made by using the  ConfTool Conference Administration System. You have to create an user account before you can submit contributions or register as a participant. The deadline for Abstract Submission is March 18, 2017.

Payment and Conference Fees


Payment is made using the Paypall system. The Paypall rules are different for each country. For example, Brazilians have to validate a creditcard within the Paypall system before they can finish a payment, and this may take a few days. Please consider to create and validate your Paypall account with at least three days in advance. The Conference Fees below are in Real (R$ or BRL), the Brazilian currency.
