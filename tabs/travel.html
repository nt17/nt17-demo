TRAVELLING TO ISTANBUL

BY AIR

There are daily direct flights from all major cities of the world to Istanbul airports. The prices vary depending on the time of booking. We therefore strongly suggest our guests to book their flights long in advance due to Istanbul air traffic is very busy in September.

*Official Airline Sponsor

Turkish Airlines is the official airline of “10th European Conference on Software Architecture (ECSA 2016)” and special discounts are offered on certain booking classes. In order to proceed with the online booking tool for Turkish Conventions please visit the Turkish Conventions website and use the event code “170TKM16” under delegate section.

BY SEA

There are daily ferries from some Greek Islands and Black Sea ports. The cruise boats call Istanbul regularly until the end of October.

BY CAR

Guests coming from East Europe countries can enter Turkey from Greek & Bulgarian border gates of Turkey. There are regular train and coach lines from major European cities.
