Travelling to Belo Horizonte


Air Transport
The city of Belo Horizonte is served by two airports: Confins International Airport (CNF), with domestic and international flights, and Pampulha (PLU) with domestic flights only.

Confins International Airport (CNF)
CNF is situated in the Belo Horizonte metropolitan area, in the municipality of Confins. The estimated time from the airport to the Conference location (Pampulha) is about 40 minute by car.
Address: Rodovia LMG 800, KM 7.9 Confins – MG, 33500-900
More information: International Airport
The transfer from the CNF airport to the Conference location (Pampulha) can be made using taxi (you will pay around 110 Brazilian Reais), or taking a bus to the Pampulha (PLU) airport (the bus ticket is around 15 Brazilian Reais). From the Pampulha airport, you can take a taxi to your hotel or to the conference site. You will pay something around 15-25 Brazilian Reais. Or you can walk – check distances on google maps (click here for PLU at google maps).

Pampulha Airport (PLU)
PLU is located near the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG) - about 5 minutes by car.
Address: Praça Bagatelle, 204 – Bairro São Luiz -Belo Horizonte – MG. CEP: 31270-705
More Information: Pampulha Airport
