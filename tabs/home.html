Welcome to Belo Horizonte for NT17!


The NT conference meetings disseminate the latest advances in the science and applications of low dimensional systems, including carbon nanotubes, graphene and others.

The eighteenth edition – NT17 – will be held on June 25-30, 2017, at UFMG, Belo Horizonte, Brazil.

We look forward to welcome you in Belo Horizonte.
