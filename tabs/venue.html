VENUE

ECSA2016 will be organized at the Atakoy Campus in Istanbul Kultur University, Istanbul. Apart from having excellent conferencing facilities, the Atakoy campus of Istanbul Kultur University is easily accessible from the International airport in Istanbul. The Yenibosna metro station, bus and metrobus stations are very close to the Atakoy Campus. It is located on the E-5 Highway, and it takes 10 minutes to get to International Atatürk Airport and Bakırkoy Seabus Port.

The delegates arriving at the International Atatürk Airport can use the subway system. Station is inside the airport. The delegates can use the metro and get off the train in Yenibosna station which is only two stations away from the airport. University can be reached within 10 minutes of walk from the Yenibosna Metro Station.
