Satellite Symposia


Five Satellite Symposia will take place after the main conference on Friday, June 30:


  • CCTN17: 12th International Symposium on Computational Challenges and Tools for Nanotubes

  • MSIN17: 11th International Workshop on Metrology, Standardization and Industrial Quality of Nanotubes

  • CNBMT17: 8th Symposium on Carbon Nanomaterials Biology, Medicine & Toxicology

  • GSS17: 8th Graphene and 2D Materials Satellite Symposium

  • CNTFA17: 5th Carbon Nanotube Thin Film Electronics and Applications Satellite
